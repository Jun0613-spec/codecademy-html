About Us

The Irish Whiskey Lads Powered by Community We built an Irish whiskey Lads that brings people together via great whiskey experiences, unique whiskey releases and remarkable cask programs. We believe that Irish whiskey brings people together and when that happens stories are told, sips are shared and friendships are made.

If you're looking for a fun and learning about Irish Whskey. this is the club for you. Feel free to get in touch and check out our events.

Irish Whiskey Tasting Events

Each quarter, we'll invite Irish whiskey distillers and producers to lead a tasting of one or more of their very own Irish whiskeys. Tasting Kits will be available to members who wish to sample each whiskey during a private tasting event
